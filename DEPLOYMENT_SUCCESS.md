### How to Add Environment Variables

1. **Go to Vercel Dashboard**
   - URL: https://vercel.com/rajshah9305s-projects/newnocoderaiapp

   - In the top navigation

3. **Click "Environment Variables"**
   - In the left sidebar

   - Name: `VITE_GEMINI_API_KEY`
